Product Requirements Document: DapsiGames
________________


1. Overview
App/Website Name: DapsiGames
Description:
 DapsiGames is a Study + Productivity Hub that transforms studying into a gamified experience for students. It rewards users with XP points, badges, and leaderboard rankings for completing study tasks, staying focused, and meeting goals — merging productivity with fun competition.
The platform is designed to help students boost focus, track progress, and stay motivated while studying. With built-in Pomodoro timers, leaderboards, and digital planners, DapsiGames redefines how learners engage with their studies.
Primary Target Audience:
 Students aged 13–25 (high school to university level) who seek engaging, structured, and goal-oriented study experiences.
Purpose:
 To motivate students through gamification, progress tracking, and rewards — making learning both productive and entertaining.

7. User Flow
New User Journey:
                                                                                                                     1. Visits homepage and signs up.

                                                                                                                     2. Verifies email → Redirects to onboarding tutorial.

                                                                                                                     3. Lands on Dashboard with 0 XP.

                                                                                                                     4. Starts first focus session via Pomodoro timer.

                                                                                                                     5. Completes task → earns XP → visible on leaderboard.

                                                                                                                     6. Unlocks first badge (“First Focus”).

                                                                                                                     7. Returns daily to maintain streak and climb ranks.

Returning User Journey:
                                                                                                                        1. Logs in → Dashboard loads with updated stats.

                                                                                                                        2. Checks leaderboard rank and new achievements.

                                                                                                                        3. Adds new tasks or continues existing goals.

                                                                                                                        4. Completes sessions and earns XP automatically.

The implementation is divided into 8 structured phases, ensuring modular, scalable, and test-driven development.
